F.A.Q.

What is competition score ?

Competition score is the normalized ratio of job applicants for a job position. Lower value means you would compete with fewer applicants. But it does not mean that preparing for job itself is easy !


Is this information legit ?

For the most part, Yes! We try our best to collect the data from various trusted sources. It may not always be exact, but its good enough to keep you informed about latest job intel.


Why ?

Saturation is the curse that CS students in India suffer from, and AI has made it even worse. 1000+ applicants for 1 position is enough to make anyone hopeless. unSaturate exists to help you find the right CS sub-field.


What is saturation ?

Saturation is a situation where a field (Computer Science) becomes overly crowded with applicants, leading to increased competition and lower chances of getting hired. In other words, supply > demand.
